About the Author

This article was written by Charles Iliya Krempeaux.
You can reach him on the Fediverse at @reiver@mastodon.social.

Charles Iliya Krempeaux has been an ActivityPub developer since the year 2021, and is an active member of the Fediverse developer community. He created the second hosting company on the Fediverse — SpaceHost — and as a result has a deep understanding of hosting Fediverse servers. He has worked on a number of ActivityPub server software and client apps. And, he implemented the ActivityPub protocol from scatch — in the Go programming language (golang). Charles is also the organizer and founder of the Fediverse conference: FediCon. Charles coined the name “ActivityHTML” to describe using HTML (rather than JSON-LD) as a wire-format for ActivityPub.
